Current Openings

We're Looking For You

No formal degree? No problem. We value skill, dedication and willingness to learn above all else.

Panel Wiring Technician

Full Time · On-site

Wire and assemble electrical panels under supervision. Basic electrical knowledge preferred but not required — we train.

  • ITI / Diploma in Electrical (preferred)
  • Willing to learn wiring & assembly
  • Punctual and hardworking
Apply

Sheet Metal Fabricator

Full Time · On-site

Cut, bend and fabricate MS sheet metal enclosures for electrical panels. Experience with fabrication tools is a plus.

  • Experience with metal work / welding
  • Physically fit and detail-oriented
  • Minimum 10th pass
Apply

Powder Coating Operator

Full Time · On-site

Operate powder coating equipment to finish panel enclosures. Training provided for the right candidate.

  • Any prior painting/coating experience
  • Eye for quality finish
  • Team player
Apply

Quality Control Helper

Full Time · On-site

Assist in checking and testing completed panels before dispatch. Attention to detail is the most important skill here.

  • Basic understanding of electrical work
  • Careful and methodical worker
  • ITI or Diploma preferred
Apply

Store & Dispatch Helper

Full Time · On-site

Manage incoming materials and outgoing panel dispatch. Involves inventory tracking and coordination with transport.

  • Basic reading/writing skills
  • Organized and responsible
  • Minimum 8th pass
