About Us

A Legacy of Electrical Excellence Since 2003

Upadhyay Brothers & Co. was established in 2003 by the visionary Mr. Tribhuvan Upadhyay in Mandhana, Kanpur Nagar. With a passion for quality engineering and a commitment to industrial growth, the company quickly earned its reputation as a trusted name in electrical panel manufacturing across Uttar Pradesh.


Since 2017, the company has been ably led by Mr. G.K. Upadhyay, who has expanded operations and strengthened the company's in-house manufacturing and Powder Coating units. Today, UBC boasts a team of professionally qualified engineers, skilled supervisors, and dedicated supporting staff, all working from their production facility in Mandhana — ensuring every panel delivered meets the highest standards of quality and safety.

Motor Control Centre (MCC) Panel

Main & APFC Panel
